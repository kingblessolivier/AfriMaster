Our Story

Who We Are

A user-friendly platform that streamlines property management with efficiency, transparency, and innovation — built right here in Rwanda.

Our Mission

To simplify property management and create a seamless experience for every stakeholder. We foster trust and transparency in real estate, equipping owners, tenants, and agents with the tools they need to succeed — all in one place.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PropertyHub was born from a simple observation: property management in Rwanda was fragmented, opaque, and frustrating for everyone involved. Our founders built a better way — and haven't stopped improving it since.

Why Choose Us

A Platform Built with You in Mind

We understand the challenges of the Rwandan real estate market. PropertyHub was designed from the ground up to address them — combining local expertise with world-class technology.
